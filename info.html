About Us

Countrify is a B2B e-commerce marketplace built by Hexlet Private Limited. We connect wholesalers (sellers) and retailers (buyers) on a mobile-first platform to make wholesale buying easy, fast, and secure. Our goal is to empower businesses with technology that helps them grow, trade efficiently, and reach more customers across India.

Countrify Marketplace Profile

Countrify is designed for bulk transactions between verified wholesalers and retailers. Sellers can list their product catalogs, manage orders, and receive payments securely. Buyers can explore multiple categories, place large orders, and track deliveries—all in one app. We focus on trusted trade, transparent pricing, and smooth logistics to support India’s growing wholesale ecosystem.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed in cases such as order cancellation, item not delivered, or delivery of wrong/damaged goods. Buyers must raise refund requests within the specified window. Once verified, refunds are processed to the original payment method or escrow, as applicable. Sellers may also be contacted during the resolution process.
